  • Welcome to OWLAND

    Owland is an exciting interactive video game designed to assist students in making informed decisions about their high school education.

    In our virtual campuses, students will be able to explore a wide range of academic subjects and future job opportunities through non-player characters.

    What Problem Did We Find?

    For a long time, our team has observed a problem in the current education system. This problem is the lack of information regarding the choice of majors in our academic system, limiting the possibilities for students to maximize their potential by selecting a major that aligns with their abilities and preferences.

    How Can It Affect Us?

    The lack of information can greatly influence one of the most important decisions in our lives, which is choosing a major. If we don't choose one that allows us to unleash our full potential, it can harm our future and close many paths to possible careers or ambitions.

    Who Are We?

    We are a group of young students with strong values ​​of excellence, self-improvement, leadership, solidarity, and integrity. With these values, we aim to assist future professionals as much as possible by providing tools that enhance their best decision-making.
